How long does a website take?+
Most business websites take 3–5 weeks from kickoff to launch; larger custom builds run 6–10 weeks depending on scope.
How much does software cost?+
It depends on scope — a landing page starts far lower than a hospital ERP. Use our project estimator on the Contact page for a quick range.
Do you provide maintenance?+
Yes — hosting, updates, monitoring and support plans are available for every product we build.
Can you build custom ERP?+
Yes, including School ERP, Hospital ERP, CRM, billing, inventory and POS systems tailored to your workflow.
Can you develop AI solutions?+
Yes — chatbots, workflow automation, OpenAI/Claude/Gemini integrations and predictive analytics are all part of Rarebreed AI.

Yes, through Rarebreed Academy — see our Careers page for open internship tracks.

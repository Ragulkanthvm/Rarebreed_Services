Problem

Patient records, billing and pharmacy stock were tracked across three disconnected tools, causing daily reconciliation errors.

Solution

Technology

React, Node.js, PostgreSQL, deployed on AWS with role-based access control and audit logging.

Result

Zero-downtime rollout, reconciliation time cut from hours to minutes, and full audit trail for compliance.
